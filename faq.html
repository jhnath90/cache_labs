Below is a list of commonly asked questions. Contact us if you don't find an answer to your question below.

  • How much does the course cost? - $6,000

  • How long is the course? - 144 hours (12 weeks / 12 hours per week)

  • Will we build a portfolio? - Yes

  • Do you guarantee a job upon graduation? - No

  • Do I need a computer to attend this course? - Yes. We prefer MacOS or Linux. You can use Windows but you will encounter more issues than someone with a Mac or someone running Linux.

  • Why should I attend a bootcamp instead of teaching myself? - Some people can absolutely teach themselves all the concepts covered in our course. However, most people will hit a wall at some point and need further assistance. Think of this like learning to play basketball. If youre coach tells you that you need to work on your shooting mechanics then you know you need to go home and practice your shot form. If someone tells you that they want you to make a web app that does x, y and z or that they want a website that looks a certain way then you most likely won't know where to start to accomplish the tasks necessary to complete these projects. You don't know what you don't know and that's where our course can help.

  • How can I learn all this in 12 weeks? - You can't stop learning when you leave class. We will only have 144 hours of in class time together. You need to take what you learn in class and then practice that outside of class. There will be homework assignements given at the end of every class and extra resources for you to utilize outside of the classroom. To give some perspective the average amount of time spent in 1 collegiate level class is 42 hours per semester so taking this course is as much classroom time as almost 3.5 college courses, that's almost a full semester.
